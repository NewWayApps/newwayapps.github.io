Privacy Policy for Puzzle Time

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Newwayapps ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, Puzzle Time (the "App").

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Puzzle Time is designed to be a 100% offline application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not track your usage behavior or device information.

3. Local Image Processing

A core feature of Puzzle Time allows you to create custom jigsaw puzzles using photos stored on your device. These images never leave your device. All processing required to turn your photos into puzzles happens locally. We do not upload your photos to any external servers, and we have no access to them.

4. Third-Party Services

Because the App operates completely offline, it does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising networks that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Puzzle Time is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about our offline practices, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at hello.newwayapps@gmail.com.
